## Unique Features

### Deep Research
Gemini Deep Research autonomously searches for 20-30 minutes on complex topics.
Use it for market research, literature reviews, competitive intelligence.

### NotebookLM
Upload your documents and ask:
- 'What are the key themes across all my research papers?'
- 'What contradictions exist between Document A and Document B?'
- 'Create a study guide from my notes'

### Google Sheets Integration
Use Gemini formulas directly in Sheets for bulk AI processing.
